Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need to install anything?

No. The bot runs entirely on our servers. You control it from any browser — phone, tablet, or desktop. Nothing to download.

Is my game account safe?

Each account runs in its own isolated emulator instance. We log in using a one-time code from the game — we never see your email or password. The code expires after a single use.

How long until my bot is running?

After you subscribe and send us your game info, we typically have your instance set up within 24 hours. Often sooner.

Can I share access with alliance members?

Yes. You can grant read-only or full control access per device. Each person gets their own login — no need to share passwords.

What if I want to play manually?

Just stop the auto-modes from your dashboard. You can also log into the game on your phone at the same time — the bot will gracefully pause if it detects you're active.

Can I run multiple accounts?

Each subscription covers one game account. Subscribe again for each additional account you want automated.

What happens if the bot crashes?

The bot is built with automatic error recovery — it handles popups, navigation failures, disconnects, and restarts itself. If something truly unexpected happens, we monitor and fix it.

How do I cancel?

Log into the portal, go to Billing, and click Manage Subscription. Cancellation is instant through Stripe. Your bot runs until the end of the billing period.
